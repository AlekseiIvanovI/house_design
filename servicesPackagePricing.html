Services/Package Pricing

At Design Haven, we offer a range of comprehensive house design services to help you create your dream home. Our experienced team of architects and designers is dedicated to delivering exceptional design solutions that meet your unique requirements. Explore our service packages and pricing options below

Conceptual Design Package

Our conceptual design package is perfect for homeowners who are in the initial stages of designing their dream home. We work closely with you to understand your vision, lifestyle, and functional needs. Our conceptual design package includes:

  • Consultation to discuss your requirements, preferences, and design goals.
  • Site analysis and evaluation to understand the context and potential of your property.
  • Concept development, including floor plans and 3D visualizations.
  • Basic exterior elevations to give you a sense of the overall design direction.
  • Presentation of the concept with the opportunity for feedback and revisions.

Full Architectural Design Package

Our full architectural design package provides a comprehensive solution for homeowners seeking a complete set of construction drawings and detailed design specifications. This package is suitable for those who require a higher level of customization and a detailed design process. Our full architectural design package includes:

  • Detailed consultation to understand your lifestyle, design preferences, and specific requirements..
  • Concept development, including floor plans, exterior elevations, and 3D renderings.
  • Development of construction drawings, including detailed floor plans, sections, and elevations.
  • Coordination with structural engineers and other consultants, if needed.
  • Selection of materials, finishes, and fixtures to enhance the overall design.
  • Permit acquisition and assistance with the approval process.
  • Construction administration, including site visits and coordination with contractors.

Interior Design Package

Our interior design package focuses on creating beautiful and functional interior spaces that reflect your style and personality. Whether you're looking to renovate your existing home or design the interiors of a new build, our interior design package covers all aspects of interior design. Our interior design package includes:
